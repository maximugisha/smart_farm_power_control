How It Works

Simple steps to get your farm's power under control

1. Install Sensors

Attach our power monitoring sensors to your farm equipment. Each sensor connects wirelessly to the central system.

The sensors are weatherproof, durable, and designed specifically for agricultural environments.

2. Connect to Dashboard

Access your personalized web dashboard to see all connected devices and their power status.

You can monitor usage, set up alerts, and control devices from any web browser or mobile phone.

3. Optimize Your Farm

Use the insights and controls to optimize your farm's power usage. Create schedules, receive alerts, and control equipment remotely.
